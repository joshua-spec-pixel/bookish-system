Productivity-Time Dilation Calculator

About The Project

The Productivity-Time Dilation Calculator focus transforms our perception of time at work by reformulating Einstein's groundbreaking theory of time dilation.

What Problem This Solves

Project P transforms abstract productivity into hard numbers. By calculating your focus factor through the physics of time dilation, it reveals exactly how much real progress you're making, turning 'I worked all day' into measurable impact that drives results.
Formula Used:

Δt′ = Δt √(1 − v² c² ) = γΔt
Where,
Δt -> Actual time: Time spent on work
Δt′ -> Dilated time or time 'bent': Real feel of time when you achieve flow state
v² c² -> How fast you are: In actuality, this is the focus that bends time to YOUR attention and exponentiates into many hours of work done.
Replaced v² c² with Focus Factor, a number YOU choose between 0 - 0.99, depending how good was your focus (Example: see Key Screen).

Why It's Designed This Way

I had noticed that a highly focused hour of work seems to stretch longer than a distracted one.
Project P quantifies this phenomenon, inspired by the concept of time dilation from physics; except here, the 'speed' is your focus.

Features

  • Calculates perceived (dilated) work time based on your focus
  • Validates user input for realistic focus values
  • Experimental interface, inspired from Albert Einstein's Theory of Special Relativity
